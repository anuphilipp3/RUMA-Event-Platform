# RUMA Events Platform  
## Vision  
RUMA Events is a modern community event management platform designed to help resident associations organize, manage, and operate events through a simple, mobile-first experience.  
The platform eliminates manual spreadsheets, paper tickets, WhatsApp tracking, and Google Forms by providing a unified system for registrations, payment verification, digital ticketing, attendance tracking, and event analytics.  
The first implementation will support the RUMA Onam Celebration 2026, serving as the foundation for future events including Vishu, Christmas, Family Day, Sports Day, and cultural gatherings.  
  
## Problem Statement  
Community associations frequently rely on fragmented tools:  
* Google Forms for registrations  
* UPI screenshots in WhatsApp  
* Manual Excel sheets  
* Physical tickets  
* Manual attendance tracking  
* Manual lucky draw management  
These processes are time-consuming, error-prone, and difficult to manage at scale.  
  
## Solution  
A single platform that enables:  
* Event registration  
* Ticket purchase  
* Payment proof upload  
* Approval workflow  
* Digital ticket generation  
* QR-based event check-in  
* Lucky draw management  
* Event analytics  
  
## Design Principles  
## Mobile First  
Most residents will access the platform from WhatsApp on mobile devices.  
## Simplicity Over Complexity  
Every interaction should require the fewest possible steps.  
## Family-Centric Registration  
Registrations are performed at a family level while tickets are managed individually.  
## Community-Friendly  
The platform should feel welcoming, festive, and familiar.  
## Future-Proof  
The system should support multiple events without requiring redesign or redevelopment.  
  
## Success Metrics  
* 90%+ registrations completed digitally  
* 100% digital ticket generation  
* Less than 30 seconds check-in time per family  
* Zero duplicate ticket entries  
* Accurate attendance tracking  
* Reduced organizer administration effort
